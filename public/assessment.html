1 Opening

Read the following aloud to the candidate (~2 min)
Hello [Candidate], thank you for joining today. My name is [Assessor]. Today we'll go through a structured assessment that helps us understand your work style and preferences. There are no right or wrong answers — we're interested in how you naturally approach work.
The session will take about 45 minutes and has three parts: first, a set of statements where you'll rank which ones describe you best; second, some work scenarios where you'll tell me what you'd do; and third, a few interview questions about your past experiences.
Do you have any questions before we begin?
If the candidate asks what the assessment measures, say: "It looks at various aspects of your work style and personality." Do not mention conscientiousness specifically.

A Forced-Choice Personality Inventory

~10 minutes — Read each group of 3 statements aloud
In this section, I'm going to read you groups of three statements. For each group, tell me which statement is MOST like you and which is LEAST like you. The remaining one will be your middle choice.
For each block: Read all three statements. Click the rank buttons (1st, 2nd, 3rd) based on the candidate's response. 1st = Most like me, 3rd = Least like me.

B Situational Judgment Test

~12 minutes — Read each scenario and options aloud
Now I'm going to describe some work situations. For each one, I'll give you four possible actions. Please tell me which action you would be MOST LIKELY to actually take — not what you think is the "right" answer, but what you would genuinely do.

C Structured Behavioral Interview (STAR)

~18 minutes — 6 questions, ~3 minutes each
Now I'll ask you a few questions about your past work experiences. For each one, please describe a specific situation — tell me what happened, what your role was, what you actually did, and what the result was.
Allow ~3 minutes per question. Use follow-up probes if the answer is vague. Gemini will score from the transcript.

Closing

That concludes our assessment. Thank you so much for your time and your thoughtful responses, [Candidate]. We'll be in touch with next steps. Do you have any final questions for me?
